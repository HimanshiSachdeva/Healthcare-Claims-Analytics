# Healthcare Claims Analytics – Medical Malpractice Study

This project analyzes medical malpractice claims data from United Health Group to uncover key factors influencing payout amounts and legal outcomes.

## Key Objectives
- Analyze the impact of private attorney representation on payouts
- Compare payouts across physician specialties and claim severity
- Evaluate the role of insurance type in claim outcomes
- Assess the effectiveness of Medical Liability Reform (MLR)

## Methods Used
- Descriptive statistics
- Hypothesis testing (t-tests, ANOVA, Chi-square)
- Confidence intervals
- Multiple comparison analysis

## Key Insights
- Claimants with private attorneys received significantly higher payouts
- No consistent payout differences across physician specialties
- Insurance type had limited impact compared to legal representation
- Medical Liability Reform showed minimal impact on high-risk patient acceptance

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel (Statistical Analysis)
- Data Visualization
- Inferential Statistics

## Business Impact
The analysis provides actionable insights for healthcare insurers to better understand risk factors, legal dynamics, and cost drivers in malpractice claims.
